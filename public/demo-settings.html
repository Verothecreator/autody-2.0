About

Autody

Autody is being built as a serious account layer for markets, digital assets, research, and future AU utility.

Autody exists to bring crypto, stocks, ETFs, commodities, watchlists, account research, and wallet activity into one place so users can understand their money before making a move.

The larger vision is not just buying low and selling high. Autody AU is planned as a utility currency inside a broader account system, with future use for exchange, payment, goods, services, and platform activity.

The demo account helps users practice the experience before live funding, while the live account grows toward verified deposits, secure ledgers, and production-grade account controls.
