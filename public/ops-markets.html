Asset settings

Price engine

Step percent controls each tick size. Example: at 1%, a $7.00 asset can move roughly to $6.93 or $7.07 per tick. Trend bias pushes probability: -1 leans down, 0 is neutral, +1 leans up.

Reserve model affects real buy, sell, and swap impact. Example: 100,000 asset reserve and $700,000 USD reserve gives a $7 base ratio. Keep current price aligned with USD reserve / asset reserve when you want the pool ratio to be the displayed starting price. Buys lower asset reserve and raise USD reserve; sells do the reverse. Market cap is price x circulating supply. FDV is price x total supply.

24h volume is the USD trading volume shown to users for this asset. It is not AU quantity and liquidity will not overwrite it. Set it manually, or set min/max and choose how often Autody rolls a fresh volume. Use 1440 for once per day, 60 for hourly, or 10 for a faster live feel.
