A practical workflow

From headline to informed action.

Good research is not about predicting every move. It is about knowing what changed, what you hold, what can affect the asset, and what action fits your plan.

1. Find the assetCheck price, movement, category, and market details.
2. Read the storyCompare relevant business, economic, and market news.
3. Check exposureReview your wallet, available funds, position size, and watchlist.
4. Choose the next stepBuy, sell, swap, send, receive, or keep researching.

What Autody is for

A clearer home for market decisions.

Autody combines public market information with account tools for traders who want their research, watchlists, wallets, and orders close together. Market information can change quickly and is not a promise of profit or a substitute for personal financial advice.
